What Is a Business Rules Engine?

A Business Rules Engine (BRE) is a software system that allows organizations to separate business logic from application code. Instead of hard-coding decision-making logic into your applications, you can define, manage, and execute business rules through an intuitive interface.
With Code Effects, business analysts and domain experts can directly modify rules without requiring developer intervention, dramatically reducing time-to-market and eliminating bottlenecks in your development process.
Our engine evaluates rules in real-time, ensuring consistent decision-making across all your applications while providing the flexibility to adapt to changing business requirements instantly

How does a business rules engine work?

A Business Rules Engine (BRE) lets you define and manage business logic separately from application code. With Code Effects, non-developers can create and update rules through an intuitive interface—no coding required.
